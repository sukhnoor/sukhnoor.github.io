Property Management Services

Managing a property takes alot of efforts, so, to enable you to manage them peacefully and joyfully, Sukhnoor Realty Services brought you a complete property management solution, where we help individual property owners or associations to manage and maintain their porperties in a professional way. Either it's a residential property of commercial property, we have solutions for all. We bind our relationships with legal bonds to ensure that our clients get what we promise.

Accounts Management

We help associations to maintain their receipt and expenditure accounts and share the required reports with them.

Bills & Tax Payments

You need not to take the pain of paying the utility bills and property taxes as we collect and deposit them to the associated departments for you.

Care Taking

No matter wherever you are, your property is always secured and closely monitored and you will be updated regularly.

Repair Works

We manage all the repair works required at your property. Just let us know about the problem and our inhouse team of technicians will fix it.

Housekeeping & Hygiene

Our management team keeps a very close look at the tidyness of the premises to keep it hygienic and healthy.

Parking Management

We take care of the parking space allotments and also make sure that the parking policies are strictly followed.

Rental Services

We provide brokerage free rental services to all our clients wheather they are owners or tenants. Please check our Rental Services section.

Security

Along with the care taking, we ensure that the premises are secure enough with the help of security staff and CCTV Coverage.

Renovation Services

We help our clients with their renovation works with the help of our renovation associates. Best quality is delivered with lowest costs.

Association Elections & Meetings

We help associations in organizing the elections and meetings and we also ensure that the policies made are followed strictly.

Organizing Festival Celebrations

India is a land of festivals and we assist our clients in organizing the celebrations by arranging all the needs like catering, sound system, tent house etc.

Managing Legal Requirements

There is some legality is involved in property management so our legal team takes care of all that ensuring that everything is legally aggreed.
